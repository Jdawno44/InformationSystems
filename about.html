About Me

This is what I do.

My name is Jocelyn Ordyna and I love being active! Some things that love doing are playing outside, going on hikes, walking my dog, playing basketball, going running, doing high jump, gardening, playing at the beach, boating, shooting guns, throwing axes, and canoeing!

I also love doing other fun things like playing guitar and ukulele, making fun treats (especially pink heart shaped pancakes with chocolate chips), eating, reading, photography, drawing, and calligraphy. I like walking to the fridge and opening it and closing it and walking back a few minutes later and seeing if there is anything new in it.

Finally, my favorite thing to do is spend time with my family! We love to play boardgames, play Mario Kart (the best thing is when I destroy everyone), have Nerf Wars, do paintball, and make thoughtful cards. And I also enjoy making funny movies for no reason with my family and friends too!
